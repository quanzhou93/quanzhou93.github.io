Website Development Workshop

Create elegant and beautiful websites: Saturday 9/21 12:30pm-10:00 hosted by the Princeton Charter Club

Find Out More

Event Information

Website Development is a pain. We're to help. Work alongside coders who can mentor and help you shape your great idea.

This 8 hour, student organized workshop features classes for attendees with all experiences. Learn how to create the backbone of your website with HTML, splash it in style with CSS, and bring it to life with Javascript. Discover how to create dynamic websites with form control, logins, and scripts.

So, come over, and build something.

Static Websites

Simple, yet elegant. Perfect for one-page resume sites. Beauty and Elegant sites powered by Bootstrap. (2-4 hours)

Dynamic Websites

Django Functionalities including forms, internal scripts, and logins. Perfect for student organizations and start-ups. (6-8 hours)

Workshops and Talks

Student driven talks on how to create websites for both experienced and non-experienced programmers. Write your website with student Mentors there to help you. (Recommended coding level: Completed COS 126)

Itinerary

12:30pm-1:00pm
Sign-in get your wristband and see if there's anything we need to install for you.
1:00pm - 2:00pm
Introduction to Webpages: Demo on building static webpages with introduction to HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Perfect for building personal websites like these in 1-2 hours with Twitter Bootstrap Templates. Taught by Quan Zhou, MAE '15.
3:00pm - 4:00pm
Advanced Website Development: Talk on dynamic web apps that recognize individual users, read/write to databases, and scale efficiently. Develop dynamic web apps with Django, deploy them on Heroku, and enrich them with some Javascript Technologies. Taught by Shubhro Saha, COS '15.
4:30pm - 5:30pm
"How to Legally Make Six Figures Right Out College": Life as a software engineer at Microsoft and how Azure can help you host your Django website.
5:30pm - 7:30pm
Dinner: Black angus steak-burgers (Veggie burgers as vegetarian option) served by Charter Club. Yum.
10:00pm
Website Gallery: Show off your product, and talk about your experience making it. Participants win first, second, and third places with prizes from our sponsors. Enter to be part of the event raffle!
